About Me

Hi, I'm Jackie.

I live in New Hampshire, and I'm an elementary school student. I love drawing and robotics — maybe that sounds like a funny combination, but that's just me!

I hope you enjoy my drawings and my robotics adventures. This is my little corner of the internet where I share the things I love to make and do.

Whether I'm sketching a new idea or programming a robot for competition, I believe that every great thing starts with curiosity and a willingness to try.
